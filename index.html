About Us

Engineering Excellence in
Sealing Technology

Q Flow Systems is a specialized supplier of mechanical sealing solutions headquartered in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu — India's foremost industrial hub. We serve manufacturers, process plants, and OEMs who demand precision, reliability, and long service life from their sealing components.

From standard pump seals to complex custom sealing assemblies, our technical team sources and supports the full spectrum of mechanical sealing requirements across industries including chemicals, pharmaceuticals, water treatment, food processing, and heavy engineering.

Products & Services

Complete Sealing Solutions

From off-the-shelf seal components to technical consultation, we cover every stage of your sealing requirement.

Mechanical Seals

Precision-engineered mechanical seals for rotating equipment. Covering single, double, cartridge, and split seal configurations for all shaft sizes and operating conditions.

Learn more

Pump Seals

Centrifugal, submersible, and positive displacement pump seals designed to handle aggressive fluids, high pressures, and demanding temperature ranges without failure.

Learn more

Industrial Sealing Solutions

Custom sealing packages for compressors, mixers, agitators, and process equipment across chemical, pharmaceutical, food, and water treatment industries.

Seal Maintenance

Scheduled and on-call seal maintenance services to extend equipment life, minimize downtime, and ensure your plant runs at peak efficiency with minimal fluid leakage risk.
